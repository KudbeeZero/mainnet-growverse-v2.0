# MAC (Miracle Alien Cookies)

MAC, short for Miracle Alien Cookies (also called Miracle Cookies or "The MAC"), is a balanced hybrid bred by Capulator that has become one of the most coveted boutique strains in the modern cannabis market. It is prized for its glittering, resin-soaked, frosty buds and a layered terpene profile that blends bright citrus, sour diesel, creamy sweetness, and a peppery floral spice.

Effects are reported as happy, uplifting, and creative, delivering a strong mood boost and mental clarity that gradually settles into a deep, tension-melting physical relaxation. This even split between cerebral lift and body calm makes MAC popular both recreationally and among users seeking relief from stress and low mood.

THC typically tests in the low-to-mid 20s, with elite phenotypes reportedly reaching the high 20s to 30%, while CBD remains negligible. MAC's exotic reputation, award pedigree, and finicky, lower-yielding cultivation profile have cemented its status as a connoisseur strain.

## Lineage & history

MAC was created by the breeder Capulator, who combined Alien Cookies with a cross of a Colombian landrace sativa and Starfighter. Alien Cookies (itself Alien Dawg x Girl Scout Cookies) contributed the sweet, earthy cookie backbone; the Colombian landrace added uplifting cerebral energy and citrus; and Starfighter, an indica-leaning hybrid, supplied structure, body effects, and heavy resin production. The standout selection became known as "Miracle 15," from which the famed MAC1 cut and numbered phenotypes (MAC #4, etc.) were derived. Originally circulated as a near clone-only elite cut, it gained cult status before seed versions (MAC V2, etc.) were released. It earned recognition at the 2019 Emerald Cup and the 2019 Karma Cup.

## Growing notes

MAC is generally rated intermediate-to-advanced to grow. It tends toward slow vegetative growth and can stretch tall (up to several feet outdoors), so training methods such as topping, LST, and SCROG help manage height and improve canopy light penetration. Indoor flowering runs roughly 8-10 weeks (about 56-70 days). Yields are modest, commonly around 400-500 g/m² indoors, trading bulk for exceptional bag appeal and potency. It performs best indoors where light, humidity, and temperature are tightly controlled, rewarding attentive growers with dense, frosty, resin-coated flowers.
