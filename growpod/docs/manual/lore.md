## 🌌 The premise

Earth's soil ran thin. Climate swings made open-field cultivation a gamble, and
the old industry collapsed under its own weight. But the craft never died — it
**went up**.

**Orbit One** is the first orbital greenhouse: a ring of sealed growing **pods**
turning slowly against the dark, each one a self-contained ecosystem of light,
water, air, and genetics. Up here, every variable is yours to control — and every
mistake is yours to own. There's no weather to hide behind (except the kind the
station's own systems throw at you), no soil to fall back on. Just you, your
genetics, and the unblinking math of growth.

You are a **pod operator** — a grower, a breeder, a geneticist, and, if you're
good enough, the founder of an **empire** of true-breeding lines whose names are
minted permanently into the chain.

---

## 🛰️ Your mission

> **Grow. Breed. Stabilize. Mint. Endure.**

1. **Cultivate.** Raise plants from seed to harvest in a living, real-time
   simulation. Read the plant. Feel its rhythm. Keep it green.
2. **Engineer.** Cross genetics with real inheritance — dominance, segregation,
   the slow art of pulling a trait you want out of the chaos of an F1.
3. **Stabilize.** Tame a wild cross over generations until it breeds true. This is
   the grower's highest craft: turning a lucky roll into a reliable legend.
4. **Immortalize.** Mint your finest stabilized lines as on-chain NFTs — proof,
   forever, that *you* made this.
5. **Build the Empire.** Stack pods, compound your genetics, top the
   leaderboards, and write your house into the catalog.

## ✨ What makes us different

Most grow-games are clickers. You tap, a number goes up, you tap again. **GrowPod
Empire is built on systems that are actually *real*:**

### 🧬 Genetics that genuinely inherit

Nine traits, dominance, codominance, segregation variance scaled by parental
instability — and it's **deterministic**. Every cross stores its RNG seed, so any
breed can be **replayed and audited**. Your legendary line isn't a cosmetic skin;
it's a genome you *engineered*, reproducible down to the gene.

### ⏱️ A simulation that lives without you

No fake timers. Plants advance via **compute-on-read catch-up** — the world keeps
ticking in fixed hourly steps whether you're watching or asleep. Come back to a
plant that genuinely grew, dried out, fought off (or lost to) pests in your
absence. The same wall-clock moment always yields the same state: **a living world
that's also perfectly fair.**

### 💰 An economy you can audit to the cent

Every GROW you've ever earned or spent is one **append-only ledger row** with a
balance snapshot. No hand-waved currency. A built-in **burn** keeps it sound. It's
an economy designed like a real one — and it rewards **producers, not flippers.**

### 💎 Ownership that outlives the session

Your rarest, truest lines become **Algorand ARC-3 NFTs**. The database stays
authoritative — the chain is the **trophy case**, a permanent, portable record of
what you achieved. Stabilize a legend and it's *yours*, on-chain, forever.

### 🪐 Depth without busywork

Weather events, pod automation tiers, NPC contracts, auctions, leaderboards,
achievements — a full game loop, not a single mechanic stretched thin. And Pro
pods automate the tedium so the *interesting* decisions stay in your hands.

## 🏛️ The houses (player legacy)

There are no NPC factions to grind reputation with. The factions are **you and
every other operator**. Your "house" is the set of stabilized lines bearing your
name in the catalog and on-chain. The leaderboards are the politics:

- 💰 **The Magnates** — richest operators, masters of the market.
- 🧬 **The Geneticists** — top breeders, authors of new genomes.
- 🌿 **The Cultivators** — biggest single harvests, kings of yield.
- ⭐ **The Veterans** — highest level, deepest in the craft.

Pick your path. Or chase all four. The orbit is big enough for an empire.
