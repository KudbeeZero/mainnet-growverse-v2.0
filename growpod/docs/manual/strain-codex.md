## 📖 How to read a codex entry

Every strain has **visible** traits (shown in-game, drive value) and **hidden**
traits (resistances + vigor, silently shape the simulation). All inherit through
breeding.

| Stat | Meaning | Range |
|---|---|---|
| **Rarity** | Tier — drives seed cost & sale multiplier | common → legendary |
| **Stability** | How true it breeds (1.0 = landrace) | 0–1 |
| **Indica** | Indica ratio (1.0 = pure indica) | 0–1 |
| **THC / CBD** | Cannabinoid % | THC 0–35, CBD 0–25 |
| **Flower** | Flowering-stage length (days) | 45–120 |
| **Yield** | Base harvest weight (g) | 50–800 |
| **Diff** | Grow difficulty (1 = easy) | 1–5 |
| **🛡️ Dis / 🐛 Pest / ⚡ Vigor** | Hidden resistances & hardiness | 0–1 |

Seed cost by rarity: **common 25 · uncommon 62.5 · rare 150 · epic 375 ·
legendary 1000**. Sale rarity multiplier: **1.0 / 1.4 / 2.2 / 4.0 / 8.0**.

## 📜 Codex entries

### 🟦 Landraces (stability 1.0 — the foundation)

> Landraces breed **true** — zero segregation when crossed with each other. They
> are your most reliable breeding anchors and the cleanest path to a stable F1.

**01 · Afghani** — *pure indica, the tank.*
`indica 1.0 · THC 18 · CBD 4 · flower 56d · yield 500 · diff 2`
🛡️0.8 🐛0.7 ⚡0.7 · terpenes: myrcene, caryophyllene · dominant: indica_ratio,
disease_resistance. The best all-round resistances in the vault and a true-breeding
landrace — an ideal hardy breeding anchor.

**02 · Durban Poison** — *pure sativa, pest-proof.*
`indica 0.0 · THC 20 · CBD 1 · flower 63d · yield 450 · diff 2`
🛡️0.7 🐛0.8 ⚡0.8 · terpenes: terpinolene, ocimene · dominant: indica_ratio,
pest_resistance, vigor. Top pest resistance and vigor; the sativa counterpart to
Afghani for landrace crosses.

**03 · Hindu Kush** — *the beginner's dream.*
`indica 1.0 · THC 17 · CBD 3 · flower 55d · yield 420 · diff 1`
🛡️0.8 🐛0.7 ⚡0.6 · terpenes: myrcene, limonene · dominant: indica_ratio. Easiest
grow with strong disease resistance and a short flower — the textbook first plant.

**04 · Thai** — *the high-octane sativa.*
`indica 0.05 · THC 22 · CBD 0.5 · flower 95d · yield 400 · diff 4`
🛡️0.6 🐛0.6 ⚡0.9 · terpenes: limonene, pinene · dominant: vigor. High THC and
vigor, but a punishing **95-day flower** and difficulty 4 — an expert's strain.

### 🟦 Premium hybrids (rare)

**07 · Haze** — *sativa connoisseur.*
`indica 0.1 · THC 23 · CBD 0.5 · flower 84d · yield 450 · diff 4`
🛡️0.5 🐛0.5 ⚡0.9 · dominant: thc, vigor. Big THC and vigor; long flower, low
resistances — reward for skilled growers.

**13 · Girl Scout Cookies** — *elite breeding stock.*
`indica 0.6 · THC 25 · CBD 0.5 · flower 63d · yield 450 · diff 3`
🛡️0.5 🐛0.5 ⚡0.7 · dominant: thc. THC-dominant powerhouse; pair with GG#4 to
chase epic-tier potency.

**14 · Gorilla Glue No. 4** — *the potency king.*
`indica 0.5 · THC 27 · CBD 0.3 · flower 63d · yield 550 · diff 3`
🛡️0.5 🐛0.5 ⚡0.8 · dominant: thc, yield. **Highest founder THC** *and* 550 g
yield, with both traits dominant — the single best breeding chassis for climbing
rarity tiers.

### 🟩 Workhorse hybrids (uncommon)

**05 · Northern Lights** — `indica 0.9 · THC 18 · flower 49d · yield 500 · diff 1`
🛡️0.7 🐛0.6 ⚡0.7. **Shortest flower in the vault (49d)**, easy, and high-yield —
the efficiency pick. Dominant: indica_ratio.

**06 · Skunk No. 1** — `indica 0.65 · THC 16 · flower 56d · yield 550 · diff 1`
🛡️0.6 🐛0.6 ⚡0.8. Easy + 550 g + dominant **vigor and yield** — a forgiving mass
producer and great yield-donor parent.

**09 · OG Kush** — `indica 0.55 · THC 22 · flower 63d · yield 450 · diff 3`
🛡️0.5 🐛0.5 ⚡0.7. THC-dominant classic; moderate difficulty.

**10 · Sour Diesel** — `indica 0.3 · THC 21 · flower 77d · yield 500 · diff 3`
🛡️0.5 🐛0.6 ⚡0.85. Dominant thc + vigor; long flower.

**12 · Granddaddy Purple** — `indica 0.85 · THC 18 · flower 60d · yield 480 · diff 2`
🛡️0.6 🐛0.6 ⚡0.7. Indica-dominant, easy, balanced.

**15 · Jack Herer** — `indica 0.4 · THC 20 · flower 70d · yield 500 · diff 3`
🛡️0.6 🐛0.6 ⚡0.85. Vigor-dominant balanced hybrid.

### ⬜ Accessible classics (common — cheap seeds @ 25)

**08 · Blue Dream** — `indica 0.4 · THC 19 · flower 67d · yield 600 · diff 2`
🛡️0.6 🐛0.6 ⚡0.8. **Highest yield in the entire vault (600 g)** at common-seed
price — the best raw-weight value and a top yield-donor for breeding. Dominant:
yield.

**11 · White Widow** — `indica 0.5 · THC 19 · flower 60d · yield 500 · diff 2`
🛡️0.7 🐛0.6 ⚡0.75. No dominant traits → blends evenly in crosses, with solid
disease resistance. A clean, neutral breeding partner.

**16 · Pineapple Express** — `indica 0.4 · THC 20 · flower 63d · yield 550 · diff 2`
🛡️0.6 🐛0.6 ⚡0.8. No dominant traits; well-rounded and cheap — a great everyday
grow and neutral breeding base.

## 🌿 Terpene index

Terpenes are flavor/aroma flags that carry through breeding (cosmetic + future
content). The founders span:

`myrcene` · `caryophyllene` · `limonene` · `pinene` · `terpinolene` · `ocimene` ·
`humulene`

Most common: **caryophyllene** and **myrcene** (8+ strains each). Rarest:
**humulene** (only Girl Scout Cookies) and **ocimene** (only Durban Poison).
